**Positioning:** Field intelligence for serious young soccer players and the adults around them. "A calmer system for serious soccer." Stripe/Linear/Huly/Resend-tier polish applied to a youth-sports space dominated by templatey SaaS and loud hype brands.

**Why this matters:** Visual quality is the first trust signal for a 15–17yr ambitious athlete AND for the parent who pays. A generic template undersells the product and caps acquisition/valuation upside.

**Emotional target:** Confidence without pressure. Progress without perfectionism. Data without coldness. Soccer seriousness without pro-club exclusivity. Parent trust without overreach.

**Color system (locked):**
- Pitch noir `#06100b` — dominant dark surface, focus decision moments
- Chalk `#f4f6ef` — warm off-white paper, calm sections
- Signal lime `#b5ff5d` — PRIMARY ACTION ONLY. Not decoration. One accent per viewport maximum.
- Ice `#cfe6f2` — secondary analysis tone for data chrome
- Recovery amber `#f5c36e` — recovery/caution signal only
- Pitch 70/50/30 greys for hierarchy on dark; `#07110d` variants on light

**Typography:**
- Display: Space Grotesk, weight 500-600, letterspacing -0.03em at 6xl+
- Body: Manrope, 400/500/700
- Kicker: Manrope 700/800, uppercase, tracking 0.16em+, color-coded by section

**Logo:** Custom chevron/play-vector — a filled triangular play-shape bisected by a precision line reading as "direction + field line." Not a soccer ball. Reads at 16px.

**Motion language:**
- Easing `cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)` standard
- Reveal: y+26px fade, 0.52s
- Scroll-linked: vertical rail scaleY, parallax widget offset, bar fills tied to scrollYProgress
- Cursor: fluid comet — small dot + lagged ring with momentum, no flashlight gradient
- Micro-interactions: 180-220ms, translate-y-0.5 on hover for primary CTAs

**Banned tells (must never appear in copy or design):**
- Hype adjectives: revolutionary, game-changing, next-gen, breakthrough, world-class, cutting-edge
- Soccer ball imagery in logos or primary art
- Generic stock "clean modern professional" design-speak
- Medical/therapy/recruiting guarantees
- Parent-surveillance language
- AI replaces coaches claims
- "Dive into," "delve into," "seamless," "empower"

**Signature compositional rule:** One dominant visual idea per viewport. Type behaves like a brand asset. Negative space is active. Accent color earns attention; never sprays it.
